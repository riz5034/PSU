Customers

Click on the customers button, and a table of current customers saved into the Cognito user pool will be displayed. All the customer's information that is saved that was updated via their account page with all the same fields is shown to the administrator. To check on the status of the customer's account to scroll the table to the right and check the status to see if confirmed or unconfirmed.

Yes, you can do it by filling out all the fields, after clicking on the Create button below the fields. This will be done seeing you entered in all the field information correctly. The new customer account will be saved to the Cognito user pool, and the view customers table should update with that new account.

Click on the Search tab on the right side of the page. Type in an field and click on search and right below it it should filter out the results of what was searched in the table.

Click on the Update tab, search for the customer id by looking through the table of customers. Enter in the information in the fields in which you want to update, then click on the update button below the fields to update.

Click on the Delete tab, enter in the email of the account you want to delete then click the delete button. The email of the account created should then be deleted in the customers Cognito user pool.
